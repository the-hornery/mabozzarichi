Concept • Satire • Bedroom-to-barricade

Western puritanism is back. This is the counter‑spell.

Western culture is spiralling back into puritanism—clamping down on sexual expression, shaming sex workers, policing women’s choices, banning access to care. Meanwhile, predators thrive in silence, shielded by the same polite society that shuns open conversations about pleasure and consent. This album is not simply a collection of stories about finance, corruption, and sexual deviancy. It is a conceptual, metaphorical account of a man's internal war with love, class and power structures.

Who is Mabozza?

Mabozza Blurb: Part burlesque MC, part radical armchair contrarian, part work-release stripper in a thrifted catsuit. Mabozza Richi is the sound of a horny 3rd wave feminist crawling out of a well, dripping with irony and ready to spit fire about cartoons, capitalism, and carnal chaos.

His genre-hopping tracks swing between seduction and scolding, between bedroom confession and barricade chant. Drawing from a long British tradition of truth-telling through absurdity, Mabozza’s mission is simple: call out the madness, kiss it on the forehead, and sleep with its mum.
